About

Welcome to Scaleup Village Limited, a Public Limited ​Company based in Perinthalmanna for the globe. We are ​dedicated to fostering Innovation and Growth ​across multiple sectors through our ​diversified verticals: IT Park, Industrial Park, ​Startup Accelerator, Investment Funds, ​Skill Park, and our signature annual event ‘Scaleup Conclave’.

Vision

ScaleUp Village aims to create an interdisciplinary hyper-operable ecosystem for startups and existing business owners to push them to the next phase by facilitating the resources that are required to stamp their signature in the global market. Scaleup will help with physical and digital infrastructure, Mentorship support, Subject Matter Experts (SMEs), Investment opportunities, Legal counsel, CA & CS, etc, and Other support required to break out of the echo chamber and be at the forefront of the ecosystem. ScaleUp Village aims to create core startup companies with world-class standards.

Mission

ScaleUp Village aims to foster a signature bunch of startups that will be exposed to the global market. Scaleup Village will be exposing state-of-the-art facilities and resources to startups and existing business owners to push them to the next phase and thus stamp their signature in the global market. Scaleup helps to scale from their existing stage to the next horizons by helping them break out from the silos.

What We Do

At Scaleup Village, we provide a comprehensive ​ecosystem to support your business growth. ​From cutting-edge IT infrastructure and industrial ​facilities to startup acceleration, investment​opportunities, and skill development programs.

We strive to provide the right resources and help them to scale up to new horizons of global markets, We primarily help them to be presentable to the outer world with the right blend of ingredients in startups including continuous mentorship and investment support.

Startup Accelerator

Our Startup Accelerator program is designed to nurture early-stage startups by providing mentorship, workspace, and access to a network of investors and industry experts. We help startups refine their business models, scale operations, and achieve market success.

IT Park

Our IT Park offers state-of-the-art infrastructure and resources for tech companies. With modern facilities, high-speed internet, and a collaborative environment, we support the growth of IT firms and foster technological advancements.

Skill Park

Our Skill Park is dedicated to skill development and training. We offer a variety of programs and workshops to equip individuals with the skills required in today’s competitive job market, ensuring they are ready to meet industry demands.

Industrial Park

Our Industrial Park provides robust infrastructure and essential services for manufacturing and industrial enterprises. It is designed to cater to the needs of various industries, ensuring seamless operations and business expansion.

Investment Fund

Our Angel Investment Fund supports innovative startups with the capital they need to grow. We invest in high-potential ventures, offering not only financial support but also strategic guidance to ensure sustainable growth and success.

ScaleUp Conclave

Our Signature Annual event namely “Scaleup Conclave” is a premier event that brings together entrepreneurs, industry leaders, investors, and innovators. It’s a platform for networking, knowledge sharing, and exploring new vertices of business opportunities.
